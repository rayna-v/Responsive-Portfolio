About Me

Rayna J Veuleman
Jr. Web Developer

Though I am new to the world of web development, I have been unknowingly preparing all my life. With an eye for finer details and a willingness to succeed, I am a perfectionist to the core. Critical thinking, puzzles, and logic are my idea of fun. I look forward to growing and showing the world what I can do.

Who I am

I am a mother of three boys and a former math and science teacher who recently discovered a love of coding! With a logical mind and eye for details, I am so excited to begin my career as a web developer. Though I was nervous initially, it wasn't long after writing my first line of code that I knew I made the right decision. Whether it is debugging, refactoring, starting from scratch or learning something new, it scratches me where I itch.

Interests

Coding takes up much of my free-time nowadays, but I still have other things I am passionate about. I definitely consider myself to be a "foodie" who also loves hiking, camping, skiing, kickboxing, and being outdoors. Though I am not musical in anyway, music is life. Some of my favorites are: Tool, Queens of the Stone Age, Radiohead, Rage Against the Machine, Audioslave, Pink Floyd, Queen, Rush and Led Zeppelin.

Experience

I wrote my first line of code in August of 2020, but I have been immersed ever since. I have taken the first JavaScript course from FreeCodeCamp.org and am working through the Full-Stack developer bootcamp course with the University of Denver and will finish in March of 2021. Because I really enjoy what I am learning, I spend about 40 hrs a week coding, so I will be prepared to hit the ground running when I get my certificate.
