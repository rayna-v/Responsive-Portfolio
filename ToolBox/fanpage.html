Rayna's Toolbox:
A TOOL Fan Site

TOOL is often referred to as a Progressive Metal band though most would agree they are in a class of their own.
I, unfortunately, had not been introduced to TOOL until 2007. My, now husband, was a big fan and gave me the Æenima to listen to.
Never had I ever experienced music before playing that album on repeat. The more I heard it, the most I could feel it. It didn't take long
before I was hungry to hear more, and the entirety of their catalogue did not disappoint. In fact, the more I took in, the deeper my appreciation
became. TOOL is my

    Maynard James Keenan



Maynard is the most widely known member of TOOL largely due to his success with the band A Perfect Circle. He happens to be my
favorite rock singer of all time. His voice is so versatile that it almost sounds like someone different from song to song. Though
many fans may consider TOOL to be a metal band, what I love most about Maynard is his robust yell that isn't too throaty or deep
and always expertly used to create a purposeful and powerful effect.

Maynard keeps himself busy between his three main bands: TOOL, A Perfect Circle, and, the less widely known, Puscifer, but
he is also an entrepreneur who has a vineyard and winery, Caduceus Cellars, in addition to other businesses including Merkin Vineyards
Osteria, a bar/restaurant in Cottonwood, AZ; a food truck, Merkin Vineyards Wagon, and a merch store, The Puscifer Store, in
Jerome, AZ.

    Adam Jones



Adam Jones is a Grammy Award-winning guitarist who, along with Maynard and Danny Carey, has been with Tool since its conception in 1990. He is not known
necessarily for his technical ability but his unique tone and tendency to combine different techniques which adds to the overal texture of the musical experience.

It seems that his aggressive tone can be attributed to his main guitar (a 1979 Les Paul Custom Silverburst which he has several models of and has
played since TOOL's beginning) as well as his choice of amplifiers. In late June of 2020, TOOL announced that Gibson Guitars is going to release a
a signature model of his guitar to emulate the look and sound. He has used a Marshall Superbass as his primary amplifier adding a Diesel VH4 since Aenima and a
Rivera Knucklehead Tre on the latest album Fear Inoculum.

Though Adam's primary focus has always been with TOOL, he has also toured with and contributed to The Melvins which is known to have greatly
influenced TOOL's sound and style. In addition to music, he is also a gifted artist. through work such as album art, film, Hollywood makeup and set design,
photography, and comics. He is also known to be kind to fans and seems to have a great sense of humor; for Halloween 2019, he played a show as Dwight from The Office
which I think also highlights his aesthethic artistic ability.

    Justin Chancellor



Justin Chancellor was born in London, and started off his career with the Prog Metal band Peach which toured with TOOL while Paul D'Amour was still
the bassist. Though Justin enjoyed his time with Peach, he agreed to join TOOL upon Maynard's request when D'Amour quit in 1995. It took no time at all for Justin
to become one of the most iconic Æenima in 1996.
